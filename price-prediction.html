How it works

Methodology

Three inputs feed every range on this page. None of them require a paid data feed, so the model stays transparent and easy to audit.

Momentum

24-hour price change sets the directional lean — sustained moves shift the projected range up or down rather than leaving it centered on the current price.

Volatility scaling

Each timeframe widens the band using a fixed multiplier — 24 hours stays tight, a 1-year horizon allows for much larger swings either way.

Sentiment blend

The bullish/bearish/neutral badge and confidence bar reflect how far momentum has pushed the range off-center, not a guarantee of direction.
