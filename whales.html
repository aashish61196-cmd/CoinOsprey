Reading the feed

What each move usually means

Exchange Inflow

Coins moving onto an exchange from a private wallet. Often read as a precursor to selling, since coins usually need to be on an exchange to be sold.

Exchange Outflow

Coins moving off an exchange into private custody. Often read as accumulation or a shift toward longer-term holding.

Wallet-to-Wallet Transfer

Coins moving between two private wallets with no exchange involved — could be custody changes, OTC settlement, or internal reshuffling.
