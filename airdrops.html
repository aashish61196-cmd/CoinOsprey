Airdrop Safety Tips

Never share your seed phrase. No legitimate airdrop ever asks for it — that request alone is the scam.

Verify links directly. Type project URLs yourself or use official docs rather than links from DMs or comments.

Use a burner wallet. Interact with unaudited claim contracts from a wallet holding minimal funds.

Skip "pay to claim." Real airdrops don't require an upfront fee to receive free tokens.

Never miss a new airdrop

One email a week: new campaigns, closing deadlines, and which ones are worth your time.
