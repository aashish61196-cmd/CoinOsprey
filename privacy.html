Privacy Policy

This explains what information CoinOsprey collects when you use the site, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

01 Overview

CoinOsprey ("we," "us," "our") publishes crypto news, market data and research at coinosprey.com. This policy describes how we handle information from visitors, newsletter subscribers, and anyone who contacts us. It applies to this website and does not cover third-party sites we link to.

This page is provided for general information about our practices and isn't a substitute for legal advice about your specific situation or jurisdiction.

02 Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Name and email address, if you subscribe to our newsletter or submit the contact form
  • The content of any message you send us, including tips, corrections, or support requests
  • Any information you choose to include in a partnership or press inquiry

Information collected automatically

  • Device and browser type, operating system, and general location inferred from IP address
  • Pages visited, referring pages, and time spent on the site
  • Approximate reading behavior used in aggregate to understand which coverage is most useful

03 How we use information

  • Operate, maintain and improve the site, including its live price feeds and dashboards
  • Send newsletter issues and respond to messages sent through the contact form
  • Understand aggregate readership trends so we can prioritize coverage
  • Detect, prevent and address technical issues, abuse, or security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations where applicable

We do not sell personal information to data brokers.

04 Cookies & third-party services

CoinOsprey uses cookies and similar technologies for the purposes below. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.

05 Sharing & disclosure

We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help operate the site (hosting, email delivery, analytics)
  • Advertising or sponsorship partners, limited to what's needed to measure a campaign
  • Authorities, where required to comply with a valid legal request
  • A successor entity, in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

06 Data retention

We keep newsletter and contact-form information for as long as needed to respond to you and maintain our records, and delete or anonymize it when it's no longer needed for those purposes. Aggregate analytics data may be retained longer since it is not tied to an identifiable person.

07 Your rights & choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these, email privacy@coinosprey.com and we'll respond within a reasonable time.

  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter anytime using the link in any issue
  • Opt out of non-essential cookies through your browser or device settings
  • Ask what information we hold about you and request its deletion

08 Children's privacy

CoinOsprey is intended for a general adult audience and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we'll remove it.

09 International data transfers

We operate a global site, so information may be processed in countries other than your own, which may have different data protection laws. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

10 Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

11 Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.

12 Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information can be sent to privacy@coinosprey.com, or through our contact page.
